ABOUT

About the Attorney:
After completing service in the United States Army, Attorney Locus began his legal career in 1985, investigating and processing EEO claims with the Troop Support Agency in Fort Lee, VA. From 1985 until 1999, he worked as a Level IV Contracting Officer with the National Institute of Environmental Health Sciences in Research Triangle Park, North Carolina. In 1999, Mr. Locus opened his law firm full time and now practices in the areas of personal injury, employment law and contract (construction law).

James H. Locus, Jr., admitted to bar, State of Indiana, 1985; State of North Carolina, 1986. United District Court of Indiana, 1985; United District Court for the Middle District of North Carolina, 1986; United District Court for the Eastern District of North Carolina, 1986; United States Court of Appeals for the Fourth Circuit, 1990, United States Supreme Court, 1991.

Education: North Carolina A&T State University (B.S. 1980); North Carolina Central School of Law (J.D. 1983)

Membership: National Bar Association, (Life Member, 2003); North Carolina Superior Court Mediator 2007; Level IV DHHS Contracting Officer. Email Attorney Locus: locus@locuslawfirm.com
